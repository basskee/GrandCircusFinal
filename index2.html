KICKSTAND

See the city on your bike.

Discover the city with your kickstand.

Detroit's a great city and a great place to bike. But if your rides are simply taking you from start to finish, you're missing the best part about this place -- the local businesses, attractions, and people who make Detroit the unique, friendly, and awesome place that it is.

So get off your bike, use your kickstand, and experience your ride!

Get started

  • Choose a themed ride that we've pre-planned for you. Select by ride distance, time, area, or the Kickstand stops along the way.
  • Sign-in to Kickstand to enjoy the benefits offered by the businesses at many of our Kickstand stops.
  • Download the route to your portable device so you can take it with you.
  • Check the route for any special instructions or considerations regarding the Kickstand stops.

Join our community and enjoy special offers from the places along your ride where you stop and use your kickstand!
